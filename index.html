25 Words or less: A spin off

How good are your guessing skills?

Inspired by the popular gaming show "25 Words or less"!

How to play: When you click start, a 5 minute timer will begin counting down. Your first clue will automatically show up on the card and you can begin guessing, once you have made a guess make sure to click SUBMIT. If you guess right, you can move onto the next word, if not, you'll kindly recieve another clue. BE CAREFUL: for each additional clue you use, you lose 2 points. You have 300 points to start

Race against the timer whilst trying to keep all your points, once 5 minutes is up, its game over. So can you do it? There's only one way to find out! CLICK START
